Autonomous Rover

This was a group project where I helped built an autonomous rover using Arduino and Solidworks.
In this project, I was tasked with designing the rover's schematics and designing its parts on
Solidworks. My job was also to help assemble the parts and ensure the functionality was as
intended for our rover. In the end, we were able to design a fully functioning autonomous rover
that could trace through a maze on its own.
